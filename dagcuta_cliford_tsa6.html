"Familiar of Zero" (Japanese: "Zero no Tsukaima") is a Japanese light novel series written by Noboru Yamaguchi and illustrated by Eiji Usatsuka, which has been adapted into a popular anime series. The story is set in a magical world where students at the Tristain Academy of Magic summon familiars—magical creatures or beings that assist them. The plot centers around Louise Françoise Le Blanc de La Vallière, a noble girl with a reputation for failing spells, who accidentally summons a human boy from Earth named Saito Hiraga as her familiar. The series follows their adventures, exploring themes of personal growth, magic, and the evolving bond between master and familiar.

Continuation of Light Novels

The light novel series "The Familiar of Zero" is set to continue after the passing of its original author, Noboru Yamaguchi. Before his death, Yamaguchi had outlined the plot and conclusion for the remaining volumes, which will be published posthumously. This decision came in response to persistent fan requests and the desire of Yamaguchi's family to see the series completed.
